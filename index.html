Hello! My name is Daniel J Airoldi. I am an 18 year old senior at Center for Technology Essex and Essex High School, currently living in Essex Vermont. I am a beginning 3d artist and animator, and have a passion for indie games. I have a dream to be able to develop my own indie games, and puzzle people with complexe lore similar to what has captivated me with the games I am invested in.

I am currently working on models simply for the fun of it using Blender, so that way once I am required to make characters for a future game, like the one I will be making in class during the second semester. My current specialty is mainly making robots, typically FNAF style animatronics. I am going to broaden my horizons once I have less time to focus on normal school work.
